Estates & Trusts

Wills direct asset distribution after death; trusts can bypass probate, provide tax advantages, and set inheritance conditions.

A living trust holds assets during your lifetime and passes them directly to beneficiaries on death, avoiding probate and ensuring privacy.

Retirement

Funded with after-tax dollars, a Roth 401(k) allows tax- and penalty-free withdrawals of earnings if holding requirements are met.

Annuities provide guaranteed lifetime income, principal protection, and optional living benefits to help prevent outliving retirement savings.

Tax Planning

The federal gift tax applies to transfers of money or property while the donor is living; annual exclusion limits and lifetime exemptions may apply.

For 2025, IRA contribution limits are $6,500 ($7,500 if age 50 or older), subject to income thresholds for tax deductibility.

Investing

Diversification spreads investments across different asset classes to reduce risk and improve potential returns over the long term.

ETFs are baskets of securities that trade like a stock; they offer diversification, liquidity, and typically lower expense ratios.
